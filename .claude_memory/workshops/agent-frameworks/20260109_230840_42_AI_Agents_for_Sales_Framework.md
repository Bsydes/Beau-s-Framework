### Implementation Timeline
- Weeks 1-2: Foundation (core infrastructure)
- Weeks 3-4: Outreach (email, channel coordination)
- Weeks 5-6: Intelligence (qualification, research)
- Weeks 7-8: Scale (expand channels, optimization)

### Key Specs
- Email subject: 4-7 words, lowercase, no clickbait
- Email body: 50-100 words max
- Video: 45-90 seconds
- Talk ratio target: 40% rep / 60% prospect
- Hot lead threshold: Score 80+
